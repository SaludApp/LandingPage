Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 29th April 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Salud collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our website (https://salud.gift/), our mobile applications (the "Apps"), our other digital properties or partners' properties where we collect information, collectively referred to as “Services”. By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

When you use our Services, we may collect the following information:

  • Device Information: We may automatically collect certain information about your device, including, but not limited to, information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and cookies installed on your device. Additionally, we may collect information about the individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Services, and information about how you interact with our Services.
  • Personal Information: To sign up for the Apps, we may require personal information including name, phone number, email address, physical address, date of birth, profile picture, document verification, and selfies to confirm liveness. To activate a virtual card and account we may require additional information including your social security number. We may store this information for our records and share them with partners to the degree required to provide our services.
  • Phone Contacts: We may request access to and retain records of the contacts on your device to enable sending and receiving gifts through our platform.
  • Camera and Microphone: We may request access to the camera and microphone to enable customers to create personalized messages. We may store these messages and display them to the gift receivers. We may use the recordings for marketing, analysis, targeting and other purposes.
  • Order Information: When you place an order or attempt to place an order through the Services, we may collect certain information from you, including your name, billing address, shipping address, payment information, merchant, email address, phone number, transaction details and payment method information.
  • Card and Account Information: We will record information about cards and accounts generated by Salud for gifting value transfer and spend including card details, account details, transactions, and other customer activity.
  • Other Information: We may collect data from other sources such as offline data, purchased marketing data and lists.

Use of Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To fulfill your orders, including enabling the transfer of value for the purpose of gifting and enabling the value to be spent.
  • To screen users and accounts for potential fraud, money laundering, or other risky behaviors.
  • To provide you with information or advertising relating to our products or services or those of our affiliates and partners
  • To improve and optimize our products, for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with products, and to assess the success of our marketing and advertising campaigns.

Sharing of Information

We may share your information with third parties to help us use your information, as described above. We may also share your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant, or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.

We may also share information with affiliates or other parties as needed.

Behavioral Advertising

As described above, we may use your information to provide you with targeted advertisements or marketing communications we believe may be of interest to you.

Data Retention

When you interact with our products, we will retain your information as long as deemed necessary for operational, regulatory and compliance purposes.

Minors

The product is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
